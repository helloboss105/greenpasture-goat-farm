Directions

Located in the heart of Green Valley, our farm is easily accessible by car. Take Highway 123 and exit at Green Valley Road. Follow the signs for approximately 5 miles until you see our farm entrance on the right.

Parking

Free parking is available on-site for all visitors. We have designated areas for cars, buses, and larger vehicles for group visits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us, sending an email, or filling out the contact form above. We'll get back to you with availability and pricing information.

Do you offer farm tours?

Yes! We offer guided farm tours by appointment. Tours are available for individuals, families, schools, and groups. Contact us to schedule your visit.

What are your delivery options?

We offer local delivery within a 50-mile radius. For customers outside this area, we can arrange special shipping for certain products. Contact us for details.

Are your products organic?

Yes, all our products are produced using organic and sustainable farming practices. Our goats are fed natural feed and graze on pesticide-free pastures.

Can I visit the farm without an appointment?

While we welcome visitors, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure someone is available to show you around and answer your questions.

Do you offer bulk pricing?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing for bulk orders. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll provide a customized quote.
