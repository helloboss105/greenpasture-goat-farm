About GreenPasture Farm

Discover our journey of excellence in sustainable goat farming

Our Story

Founded over 15 years ago, GreenPasture Farm began as a small family venture with a simple vision: to provide the highest quality goat products while maintaining sustainable and ethical farming practices. What started with just a handful of goats has grown into a thriving operation spanning 60 acres of lush green pastures.

Our journey has been one of continuous learning, innovation, and dedication to excellence. We've combined traditional farming wisdom passed down through generations with modern agricultural techniques and veterinary science to create an environment where our goats thrive naturally.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as one of the leading goat farms in the region, serving over 500 satisfied customers and maintaining a herd of 75+ premium goats that produce the finest quality milk, ghee, and organic manure.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality goat products through sustainable farming practices while ensuring the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental stewardship. We are committed to delivering nutritious, pure, and ethically produced dairy products that enhance the health and well-being of our customers.

Our Vision

To be the leading sustainable goat farm, setting industry standards for quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility. We envision a future where our farming practices contribute to a healthier planet while providing communities with access to premium goat products.

Our Values

  • Quality and Excellence in everything we do
  • Sustainable and Ethical farming practices
  • Animal Welfare and compassionate care
  • Environmental Stewardship and conservation
  • Customer Satisfaction and trust
  • Continuous Innovation and improvement
